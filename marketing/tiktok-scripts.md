# Production Notes

**Voice:** Warm, gender-neutral preferred. Think ASMR-gentle but not whispered. Genuine, not corporate.

**Music:** Lo-fi, soft piano, or gentle ambient. No upbeat "you've got this mama!" energy. Calm.

**Pacing:** Slow. Let moments breathe. These are for tired people scrolling at 3am.

**CTA:** Soft. "Nunu is here when you need it" not "DOWNLOAD NOW"
